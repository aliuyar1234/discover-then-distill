# Submission Checklist

## Claims And Evidence

- [ ] Every claim maps to at least one result table/figure.
- [ ] Negative results and limitations are explicitly documented.
- [ ] Baselines are fair and well described.

## Reproducibility

- [ ] Commands and configs are archived.
- [ ] Seeds and environment are recorded.
- [ ] Artifact paths are stable and referenced.

## Writing Quality

- [ ] Abstract states problem, method, and concrete result.
- [ ] Introduction contributions match experiments.
- [ ] Figures and tables are legible and self-contained.
- [ ] Terminology is consistent across sections.

## Compliance

- [ ] Venue formatting rules satisfied.
- [ ] Anonymous/non-anonymous policy satisfied.
- [ ] Citation and ethics declarations complete.

## Pre-Submission Readiness

- [ ] Internal review completed.
- [ ] Final proofread completed.
- [ ] Camera-ready TODO list prepared (if accepted).
